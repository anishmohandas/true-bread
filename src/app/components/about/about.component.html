True Bread is an online Christian family magazine, published on the first day of every month. It offers reflections rooted in biblical truths, combining both devotional and theological insights through a variety of literary genres.

With a prophetic perspective on global affairs, True Bread seeks to fulfil the entrusted mission of the called-out community, shedding light on contemporary issues through the lens of Scripture. It embraces an inclusive and ecumenical Christian outlook. The magazine stands as a witness to the message of love and hope embodied in the life of the Eternal Saviour.
